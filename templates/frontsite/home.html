About us

Plaid Pearl Café is a gem situated in the heart of Greenwich Village. For 10 years is, without any doubt the best café & Sandwich shop in Kentucky. Also well known for its catering and special event platters. Plaid Pearl Café follows two simple but effective rules: Artisanal recipes and High-quality ingredients. 


This is the secret of its success and the reason why Maria Valencia, the owner, wants to export her gem to other neighbors and cities around the states. Once you can deliver constantly such a quality product it's time to grow up and expand your vision.
